About Dance at UM

Discover the thriving dance culture at the University of Michigan! Our community is home to diverse teams and styles that bring energy, creativity, and inclusivity to campus. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned performer, there's a place for everyone to express themselves.

Our Mission

At Dance at UM, we aim to unite students passionate about dance, fostering a collaborative environment where artistry and innovation thrive. Our teams embody a range of styles—from traditional to modern— showcasing the cultural and creative diversity of the Wolverine community.

Our History

The University of Michigan has a longstanding tradition of celebrating the performing arts. Over the years, our dance community has flourished into a vibrant network of teams that compete on national stages, perform at campus events, and inspire countless students to embrace their love for dance.

Why Dance?

  • Connect with a diverse group of dancers and teams.
  • Explore and master a variety of dance styles.
  • Be part of a supportive and inclusive community.
  • Perform at exciting events and competitions.
  • Grow as an artist, leader, and teammate.
